Short Bio

I am currently a Ph.d candidate at [The University of Tokyo (UTokyo)](https://www.u-tokyo.ac.jp), fortunately directed by [Dr. Zhouchen Lin](http://www.cis.pku.edu.cn/faculty/vision/zlin/zlin.htm) and [Dr. Chao Zhang](http://www.cis.pku.edu.cn/faculty/vision/zhangchao/zhangchao.htm). Before studying in NUS, I graduated from Peking University (PKU) in 2016 and during this period of time, I was fortunately directed by Dr. Zhouchen Lin and Dr. Chao Zhang. Dr. Ziwei Liu is a research fellow (2018-present) in CUHK / Multimedia Lab working with Prof. Dahua Lin. Previously, he was a post-doctoral researcher (2017-2018) in UC Berkeley / ICSI with Prof. Stella X. Yu. His research interests include computer vision, machine learning and computer graphics.

Ziwei received his Ph.D. (2013-2017) from CUHK / Multimedia Lab, advised by Prof. Xiaoou Tang and Prof. Xiaogang Wang. He is fortunate to have internships at Microsoft Research and Google Research.
