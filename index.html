Biography

I am currently a PhD student at Computer Science & Engineering Department, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), under the supervision of Prof. Jiaya Jia. Before that, I obtained the Master degree in Intelligence Science, Peking University (PKU) in 2019, supervised by Prof. Zhouchen Lin and Prof. Chao Zhang. I received the Bachelor degree in Communication Engineering from Beijing University of Posts And Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2016.

My current research interests lie primarily in computer vision and machine learning, particularly focusing on:

Imbalanced Learning, and 2D/3D Segmentation.
