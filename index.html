Biography

I am currently a third-year (2019-now) Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, the Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Jiaya Jia. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor and Master degrees from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research interests lie primarily in computer vision and machine learning. I have done some works about super-resolution, inpainting, denoising, deblurring and frame interpolation.
